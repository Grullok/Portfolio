About

I am a 23 year old freelance designer from Tallinn, Estonia who mainly specializes in designing different aspects of the world wide web.

Web design

Rimi is an Estonian store with a mobile website that I found to be somewhat difficult to use and generally not very accessible.
The buttons were small and oddly placed and there was a lot of unused screen space, whilst the rest of the design was tightly packed.

I designed a more accessible and aesthetically pleasing version of it using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InVision.
Keeping to their general design aspects, I used the space more efficiently and made navigating the website simpler.

I also designed the website and logo for a fictional restaurant using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InVision.

I aimed to give it a very fluid and flowing interface, to keep things simple and dynamic.
